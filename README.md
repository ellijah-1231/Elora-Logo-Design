# Elora – Natural & Herbal Brand Logo Design

## Overview
This project showcases the logo design created for **Elora**, a natural and herbal brand focused on purity, wellness, and nature-inspired products. The logo combines a sophisticated monogram with organic leaf elements to create a memorable and professional brand identity.

## Design Concept
The logo is designed to reflect the brand's core values:

- Natural ingredients
- Herbal wellness
- Purity and sustainability
- Premium and trustworthy branding

The elegant "E" monogram is enclosed within a circular form and enhanced with leaf motifs, symbolizing growth, freshness, and harmony with nature.

## Design Elements

### Monogram
A custom-designed "E" serves as the central visual element, creating a unique and recognizable brand mark.

### Leaf Accents
The leaves represent natural ingredients, eco-friendliness, and organic growth.

### Color Palette
- Green: Nature, health, freshness, and sustainability
- Dark Background: Premium, modern, and professional appearance

### Typography
A clean serif typeface was chosen to convey elegance, trust, and sophistication.
